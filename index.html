How to Play

🎞️

Read the clues

Every round gives you 3 emoji clues and 3 poster panels for one Telugu movie.

⌨️

Type your guess

Spelling wobbles, spacing and capitals are all forgiven — we're lenient graders.

💡

Stuck? Use a hint

Reveals Hero → Director → Year → Story hint, one at a time. Each reveal costs −3 points.

⏱️

Beat the clock

01:30 per round. The ring glows green, then amber, then red as time runs low. Run out and the movie moves on without you.

❤️

Guard your lives

You start with 3 lives. A wrong answer costs one life. Lose all three and it's game over.

🏆

Scoring

Correct +10 · Wrong −2 · Hint −3 · Skip −5. Chain correct answers for a streak.
